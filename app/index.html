Acappella Trainer

This is a lightweight web app to help train and improve your acapella singing by repeatedly lowering and then raising back the volume of a song. Test how in-tune you are with the song when the volume is lowered! Simply configure the options, and then drag and drop a .mp3 file to this page.

Acapella Trainer will initialize
seconds after pressing the play button. Volume will then fade down to
percent and stay there for
seconds before going back to
percent volume. This process repeats after
seconds.
